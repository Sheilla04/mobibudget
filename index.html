Innovation Starts Here

Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) in Kenya encounter substantial financial hurdles, primarily stemming from the high transaction fees associated with mobile money services. These fees accumulate rapidly, leading to a noticeable reduction in profit margins and posing a significant obstacle to their growth and expansion. Moreover, SMEs often lack the necessary resources and tools to efficiently mgitanage and optimize these expenses, further exacerbating their financial strain. This is where our expertise and solutions come into play. We are dedicated to assisting these businesses in navigating these challenges, providing them with the strategies and tools needed to effectively manage and reduce their mobile money transaction costs, thereby enhancing their profitability and supporting their growth ambitions.

MobiBudget

Due to the prevalent use of mobile money in Kenyan businesses, MobiBudget is in development to help SMEs manage and reduce their mobile money transaction costs efficiently. Our platform leverages data analytics and user-friendly tools to empower business owners with actionable insights, helping them navigate the complexities of mobile money transactions.

Services

Cost-Analysis Tools

Tracking, optimization, and analysis of mobile money transaction fees will be offered to SMEs. Our cost-analysis tools provide a detailed breakdown of every transaction, highlighting hidden fees and charges that can add up over time. By understanding these costs, SMEs can make more informed financial decisions. .

Tariff Comparisons

MobiBudget will compare different mobile money transaction fees of different mobile money operators for you. Our platform constantly updates with the latest tariffs from major mobile money providers like M-Pesa and Airtel Money. This allows SMEs to choose the most cost-effective operator for their specific transaction needs..

Insight Provision

This advisory tool will offer SMEs the most cost-effective methods of transacting money, increasing profitability. Based on your transaction history and patterns, MobiBudget provides personalized recommendations on how to minimize fees and optimize cash flow, ensuring your business retains more of its hard-earned money.

Financial Resources

Access to educational resources to those new to business will be available to enrich their experience in this field. Our resource library includes guides, tutorials, and best practices on financial management, tailored specifically for the Kenyan business environment. These resources help entrepreneurs build a strong foundation in financial literacy and effective cost management..
